Our Services

On-Grid Solar Rooftop Systems

An ideal solution for households, educational institutes, and industrial establishments. Features include modular design, high-efficiency inverters, and clean, silent power. Excess power can be exported to the grid.

Solar Street Lighting Systems

Standalone lighting systems for roads, townships, and rural electrification. Features include dusk-to-dawn controllers, weather-proof luminaires, and robust galvanized steel poles.

Solar Power Packs

Reliable and efficient power systems for household, campus, and garden lighting. Includes high-efficiency inverters and customizable designs to meet unique needs.

Industrial Solar Water Heating Systems

Designed for specific requirements like pressurized systems, boiler feed heating, and in-process heating. Features include automated control panels and low recurring costs.

Domestic Solar Water Heating Systems

Available in flat plate collector (FPC) and evacuated tube collector (ETC) designs, compatible with hard water. Features include PUF insulation and built-in electrical backup.
